INSTALL / GUIDE

Install NeoSpin

Follow the concise instructions for your device/browser — iOS (Safari), Chrome on iOS, or Android APK.

iOS — Add to Home Screen

Because NeoSpin isn't in the App Store, add it to your Home Screen from Safari so it runs like an app.

Download

Step B — Tap the Share button

Look for the share icon (a square with an upward arrow) at the bottom of Safari. (See image 1)

Step C — Select Add to Home Screen

Scroll the share sheet until you see Add to Home Screen and tap it. You will see a preview — tap Add to confirm.

Step D — Launch from Home Screen

Open the NeoSpin icon on your Home Screen — it will open in its own window and behave like an app.

Chrome on iOS — Add to Home Screen

If you opened this page in Chrome for iOS, tap the three-dot menu (or share) and look for Add to Home Screen. If not present, open the same URL in Safari and follow the Safari steps.

Download

Step 1 — Open the Share / Menu

Step 2 — Choose Add to Home Screen

Step 3 — Tap Add

Android — Install APK

Download and install the NeoSpin APK manually. Allow “Install unknown apps” if prompted.

Download APK
1. Tap Download APK.
2. Open your Downloads folder or tap the notification after download.
3. Allow installation from unknown sources if asked and complete install.
